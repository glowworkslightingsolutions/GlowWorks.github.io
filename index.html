About GlowWorks

Your Trusted Partner For Professional Lighting Solutions

Illuminating Southern California

At GlowWorks Lighting Solutions, we believe that exceptional lighting transforms ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences. Founded with a passion for bringing joy and wonder to every season, we specialize in creating stunning lighting displays that captivate and inspire.

Our team combines technical expertise with creative vision to deliver lighting solutions that exceed expectations. Whether it's a cozy residential holiday display, a grand corporate event, or a romantic wedding celebration, we bring the same level of professionalism and attention to detail to every project.

Service Areas: We proudly serve Orange County, Los Angeles County, and the Inland Empire, bringing professional lighting solutions to homes and businesses throughout Southern California.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know About our Lighting Services

How much do your lighting services cost?

+

Our pricing varies based on project size, complexity, and services needed. Residential projects typically range from $10-15 per foot, while commercial projects are custom quoted. We offer free consultations and detailed quotes with no hidden fees.

Do You Provide Lights or do I Need to Buy Them?

+

While We Can Hang Your Lights, We Actually Provide All Commercial-Grade LED Lights, Electrical Connections, Timers, and Installation Hardware. Everything is Included in Your Quote, You Don't Need to Purchase Anything Separately.

How Far in Advance Should I Book?

+

For Christmas Lighting, we Recommend Booking by November for First Choice and Best Availability. We Start as Early as November 1st and Can Handle a Few Clients Per Day. Halloween Projects Should be Booked by Early September. Other Events and Permanent Installations can Typically be Scheduled 4-6 Weeks in Advance.

Do You Take Down and Store the Lights?

+

Yes! We Offer Full Take-Down and Storage Services. Your Lights are Carefully Removed, Inspected, and Stored in our Climate-Controlled Facility Until Next Season. This Service is Included in Many of Our Packages and Will be Re-used as Long as Your Contract is Active, and You Can Switch Up The Custom Design Year to Year.

Are You Licensed and Insured?

+

Absolutely. GlowWorks is Fully Licensed, Bonded, and Insured With Comprehensive Liability Coverage. We Can Provide Certificates of Insurance Upon Request and all Installations Meet Local Electrical Codes.

Do You Offer Payment Plans or Financing?

+

We Require a 50% Deposit to Secure Your Installation Date and Purchase Materials, With the Balance Due Upon Completion. For Larger Commercial Projects, We Can Discuss Extended Payment Terms on a Case-by-Case Basis.

What Makes GlowWorks Different From Competitors?

+

We're a Year-Round Lighting Company, Not Just Seasonal. We Use Commercial-Grade Equipment, Provide Full-Service Support, Maintain Comprehensive Insurance, and Focus on Building Long-Term Relationships With Our Clients. Quality and Professionalism Are Our Priorities.
